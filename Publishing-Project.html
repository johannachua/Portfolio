MAGAZINE REDESIGN

Objective

The project requires to redesign an existing magazine and effectively show the principles of good editorial layout and navigation through chosen components within the magazine.

Role

Graphic Designer

Skills Gained

  • graphic design
  • layout & art direction
  • print design

About the Project

This project was created in Publishing 332, an academic course focused on the study of the design methods in print and in digital media. Through this project, I was able practice the use of following the principles of good editorial layout and navigation and to showcase my control of tone in copy and imagery.

Process & Design Rationale

For the front cover, I updated the logo to create a cleaner look to match a minimalism design approach. The logo in the redesign has more spacing between letters to be more legible and creates a more fashionable and elegant style. The headlines were made a bit bigger to increase readability. I placed some elements behind the cover artist to create some depth. As for the cover photo, I replaced it with a more vibrant photo with an interesting color palette from the photoshoot of the main feature. I chose it specifically also because it had the red flare that helps attract the eye to the magazine.

I redesigned the table of contents to help increase the hierarchy between elements. The big bold numbers help create the flow of content and assist the reader with where to look next. In addition, I placed a main leading image with different beauty products to create an appealing layout and at the same time act as a preview for one of the articles. I also included a photo for one of the main features to entice the reader and to give a glimpse of what the magazine includes. I also separated the featured content to a box-like component so that it creates its own cluster.

For the Front-of-book article, I decided to redesign the letter from the editor. I wanted to create an appealing layout so that readers would spend more time looking at the page rather than skipping the content. To do this, I replaced the images with more vibrant colored images, intricate nail art, and selected hand poses that look interesting. Overall, it made the page look trendy more attractive for the reader.
