About the Co-op

I had the opportunity to work as a marketing and design assistant during my co-op term in the summer of 2017 for abm, a biotech company. Within this job, I was able to design various promotional materials for their website and social media pages. One of my main projects within the co-op term was to create informational youtube videos.

YOUTUBE VIDEO CREATIVE PROCESS

I was responsible for completing an animated case study video to promote abm's services. This project allowed me to practice producing illustrations and animating the components rapidly while also managing multiple design tasks.

The CRISPR case study video was initially challenging and intimidating because a previous co-op student had initially started on animating the intro to the video. It was also difficult to understand the content of the script because I did not have any science background.

However, I was determined to adapt to the existing style that was created and at the same time implement the science background for the illustrations. With the help and guide of my colleagues, I was able to correctly apply the scientific content with the animations I made but also had the creative freedom that stayed within the constraints of the initial visual direction to complete the video.
